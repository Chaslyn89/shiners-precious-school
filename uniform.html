New Student Uniform Package

📦 Recommended Items

  • 2 White shirts / blouses

  • 1 School sweater
  • 1 Tracksuit
  • 2 Pairs of socks
  • 1 Pair black leather shoes

🔄 Replacement Schedule

We recommend replacing uniform items when:

  • Child outgrows current size
  • Damaged beyond repair
  • Faded or stained

⚠️ Important

All uniform items must be clearly labelled with your child's name and class.

Uniform Care Instructions

🧺 Washing

• Wash dark colors separately
• Use cold/warm water
• Avoid bleach
• Iron while slightly damp

🏷️ Labelling

• Label all items clearly
• Use permanent marker or name tags
• Include name and class
• Replace labels when faded

👞 Shoe Care

• Polish shoes weekly
• Keep laces tied
• Replace worn-out shoes promptly
• Clean mud/dirt daily
