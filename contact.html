Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Do you admit mid-term transfers? +
Yes, mid-term transfers are accepted subject to vacancy availability.
❓ What grades do you offer? +
Playgroup to Grade 9 (CBC Curriculum).
❓ How can I get fee information? +
Call or WhatsApp the admissions office at 0720 994 337.
❓ Do you provide transport? +
Yes, transport is available. Contact the school office for routes and availability.
❓ Do you provide meals? +
Yes, meals are available at an optional cost of KSh 3,900 per term.
❓ Is there a registration fee? +
Yes, admission fee is KSh 1,000 (once) for Playgroup-Grade 6, and KSh 1,500 for Grade 7-9.
