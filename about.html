About Shinners Precious School

Excellence in Education | Shaping Future Leaders

Welcome to Our School

Shinners Precious School is a leading CBC educational institution located in Embakasi, Nairobi. Established in 2009, we offer quality education from Playgroup to Grade 9, nurturing young minds with a strong foundation in academics, character, and faith. Our school is committed to providing affordable, holistic education that prepares learners for the future.

🎓 Our Mission

To provide high quality education and childcare in a safe, respectful and inclusive environment that builds a foundation for lifelong learning.

👁️ Our Vision

To be a center of excellence that produces lifelong learners and critical thinkers equipped for global success.

📜 Our Motto

Determined to win

Striving for excellence in everything we do.

Our Curriculum

📚 CBC Curriculum 💻 Digital Literacy 🇫🇷 French Language ⚽ Sports 🎭 Clubs & Activities ⛪ Christian Education

We follow the Competency Based Curriculum (CBC) which emphasizes practical learning, creativity, and critical thinking. Our learners are prepared for Junior Secondary (Grade 7-9) and beyond with a strong foundation in core subjects and essential life skills.

Our History

●
📅 2009 - School Established

Shinners Precious School was established with a vision to provide quality, affordable education to the Embakasi community.

●
📈 Growth & Expansion

Starting with Playgroup and PP1, the school has grown to offer education up to Grade 9, now serving over 500 learners.

●
🏆 Excellence in CBC

The school is recognized for excellence in CBC implementation and holistic learner development.
